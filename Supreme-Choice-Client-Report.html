Key Insight: Tier 3 Represents the Largest Growth Opportunity
Tier 3 was the top-performing source in 2024, delivering 43 policies at a $24.97 CPA with an 8.27% conversion rate. Year-to-date, bid activity on this source has dropped significantly — from a 22.2% bid rate to 7.1%. With nearly 10,000 opportunities still available this year, increasing the bid multiplier could unlock substantial additional volume from this proven source.

Key Insight: Customers 50+ Convert at 3–6× the Rate of Younger Demographics
The 60–69 age group leads with a 9.39% conversion rate and the lowest CPA at $30.99 — nearly 9× more cost-efficient than the 18–29 group ($267.90 CPA). Customers aged 50 and above represent 72% of all policies sold while making up only 59% of leads. Increasing bid modifiers for older demographics would allocate more budget toward the highest-returning segments.
